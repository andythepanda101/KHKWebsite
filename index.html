Membership

Membership is open to all CSE students. As CSE students, we realize the importance of academics, and one of the benefits of membership is having a group of friends who are studying the same material, or have taken the same classes. Besides academics, we also value social time, and have regular social events at the house

  • Learn more

House

The fraternity-owned house is located at 1100 SE 4th Street in the west end of Dinkytown. The house has plenty of social space and study space, and a large number of living spaces. It offers a great place to hang out, study, and live, along with competitive rent pricing.
